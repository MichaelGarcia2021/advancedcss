How to Make the Best Pumkin Spice Latte


What You Will Need & Steps To Make a PSL

  • Your type of Milk (I perfer Oat)
  • Pumkin Pure
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla & Pumkin Pie Spice

Making a PSL Hot


  1. Heat up all ingredients in a pot on the stove.
  2. Stir constiently.
  3. Let sit for 5 mintues.
  4. Add your choice of cream.
  5. Enjoy!

Making a PSL Cold

  • Add ice to a cup of your choice.
  • Add in all ingredients.
  • Add your choice of cream.
  • Enjoy!
